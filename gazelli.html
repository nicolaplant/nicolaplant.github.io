Talk and artist showcase at Gazelli Art House

News / 18 June, 2024

I spent a great evening at the Gazelli Art House in London on Tuesday 18 June for GAZELL.iO meetup #2, featuring talks and artworks by myself and other residency artists. I presented a new glitch version of my residency work Affective Delineation Series, an interactive VR experience exploring empathy in conversation.
