Sentient Flux to be shown for book launchat the Science Museum

News / 28 March, 2024

Coming Up

Join me at the Science Museum in London on Wednesday 8 May to celebrate the launch of new book: Robotic Vision and Virtual Interfacings: Seeing, Sensing, Shaping Edited by Luci Eldridge & Nina Trivedi Published by Edinburgh University Press, 2024.

I am excited to be showing work Sentient Flux, by myself and Alexandra Adderley, which is the focus of my chapter Embodiment and the Perception of Nonhuman Sentience in Virtual Reality Interactive Art alongside other contributors presentations and demonstrations.

6.30pm - 8.30pm, Wednesday 8 May

Dana Studio, Science Museum. 165 Queen’s Gate, South Kensington, London, SW7 5HD

Nearest tube stations: South Kensington, Gloucester Road.

The evening will include:

  • A presentation from Meg Rahaim on her chapter ‘Discrete Accidents of Photogrammetry: Re-presenting Pure Surface in Google Earth’
  • Interactive virtual reality experience Sentient Flux, which I created in collaboration with artist Alexandra Adderley, and is the focus of my chapter ‘Embodiment and the Perception of Nonhuman Sentience in Virtual Reality Interactive Art’
  • Demonstrations by Ian Dawson and Paul Reilly of the Reflective Transformation Imaging technology discussed in their chapter ‘Metalithic Postcards in the Pandemic’
  • Artworks by Kate Fahey from her chapter ‘Blinking Eyes: The Embodied Registers of Military Drone Camera Footage on YouTube’.
